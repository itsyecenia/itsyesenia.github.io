About Us

The Mother's Helper- Birthing 101 platform was created in 2020 with the aim of helping women feel empowered to choose and speak for themselves about the birthing direction they want to take. We provide information that can be valuable to any mother when making a decision for the way they want to birth their baby. This is an important step in motherhood and we want to facilatate the need to searrch for information. We are a group of educated, well research, and experiened women who run this website. We want to make sure that you recieved the most up to date information. We will provide unbiased information so that you can make the necessary choices for yourself. We are exited to begin this journey with you. We hope that all the information you find in this website is of good use and can help you choose what's best for you and your family.
