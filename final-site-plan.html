Overview

Purpose

The purpose for this website is to collect and present information to mothers about the birthing options availble to them. There will be a pros and cons list to each option with credible sources.

Audience

The audience for this website is for first time moms and moms in general who are interested in knowing all the birthing options available to them.

Typography

Heading Font: Bebas Neue

I like that the font is bold, wide, and capitalized so that it can put emphasis on it being the title.

Paragraph Font: Arapey

I chose this font because you can see a clear distinction between the heading and the paragraph. The paragraph font is simple.
